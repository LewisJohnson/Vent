Cookies

We use cookies to improve the service we bring to you. All cookies we use are set to expire a year from creation date. Here is a list of cookies we use and why.

  • ventFilters - This cookie stores your Vent filters, making sure you only see the type of posts you like.
  • allowPostComments - This cookie stores your Vent commenting preference. Whether you want comments on the posts you post or not.
